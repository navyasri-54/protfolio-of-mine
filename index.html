About

Building at the edge of logic and language

I'm a final-year B.Tech student in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning at ISTS Women's Engineering College, graduating in 2027. My work sits between two worlds — the deterministic precision of rule-based systems and the open-ended reasoning of modern LLMs — and I care most about the interface where a person meets that intelligence. My goal is simple — become an AI engineer building intelligent products people genuinely rely on.
